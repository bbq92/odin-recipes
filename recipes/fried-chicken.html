Fried Chicken

Description

Quick and easy crispy fried chicken

Ingerdients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Corn starch
  • Nandos chicken marinade

Steps

  1. Pat chicken dry and cut into 2cm bits
  2. Coat chicken in nandos marinade, set aside
  3. Start heating oil in a frypan (enough to cover chicken)
  4. Once oil is hot coat chicken in corn starch and place in hot oil. Cook for 8 minutes
  5. Once chicken is cooked remove and let cool
  6. Serve with aioli
