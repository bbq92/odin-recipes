Fried Rice

Description

Simple fried rice recipe.

Ingerdients

  • Left over rice
  • Eggs
  • Soy sauce
  • peas

Steps

  1. Cook eggs as you would a omlet then chop into strips
  2. Pour left over rice into a wok on medium heat
  3. Mix in peas and soy sauce and let heat up for 5 minutes
  4. Add egg and mix through
  5. Take off heat and serve
