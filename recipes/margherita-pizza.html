Margherita Pizza

Description

This is my recipe fo my famous margherita pizza.

This recipe has been passed down for generations. It is simple and easy to make as it only requires a few ingerdients.

Ingerdients

  • Pizza dough
  • Fresh tomato sauce
  • Fesh mozzarerlla cheese
  • Basil leaves
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Pre heat oven to 200 degeres (fanbake)
  2. Roll out dough into desierd shape
  3. Spread fresh tomato sauce all over base
  4. Rip off chunks of mozzarerlla and place around pizza then add parrmesan cheese and basil leaves
  5. Place in oven for 15 minutes
